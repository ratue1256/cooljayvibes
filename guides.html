Android to iOS

Understanding iCloud, breaking free from Google Drive, and setting up an Apple ID the right way.

1. Data Backup: Instead of relying on 'Move to iOS' which can be bugged with large WhatsApp files, manually backup Google Drive files using Google Takeout. Use WhatsApp's native iOS transfer feature.

2. Password Management: Do not trap yourself in iCloud Keychain if you ever plan to switch again. Export from Google Password Manager to ProtonPass or Bitwarden before switching.

3. Two-Factor Authentication: Before resetting your Android, transfer your Google Authenticator or Authy codes to the iOS device or use Ente Auth for cross-platform freedom.

iOS to Android

The complete guide to migrating without losing iMessage groups, photos, or your sanity.

1. The iMessage Trap: Deregister iMessage online using Apple's official tool before moving your SIM card. Otherwise, your texts from iPhone users will get stuck in Apple's servers.

2. Photos & iCloud: Use Apple's official "Transfer a copy of your data" via privacy.apple.com to directly port your entire iCloud Photos library to Google Photos overnight.

3. Notes & Reminders: Apple Notes lock-in is real. Use Google Keep or Notion. You will have to manually copy crucial notes, as Apple provides no bulk export option.
